Fraud Alert

Job Matrix Consultancy

Job Matrix is committed to maintaining transparency and protecting candidates from any form of fraud or misuse of our company name. We would like to inform all users and job seekers that Job Matrix provides interview training, resume assistance, mock interview sessions, and career guidance services. We charge candidates only for these professional preparation services and never charge any fees for job placement or employment opportunities.

Payments made by candidates are strictly related to services such as interview training, resume creation, career guidance, and mock interview sessions. Job Matrix does not collect any money for providing jobs or guaranteeing employment. Candidates should be cautious of any person or organization claiming to offer guaranteed jobs in exchange for payment in the name of Job Matrix.

Job Matrix accepts payments only through official payment channels such as our website payment gateway or verified UPI methods associated with our organization. We do not authorize any third party, agent, or individual to collect payments on our behalf. Only the official Job Matrix team communicates directly with candidates regarding services and support.

Candidates should note that all official communication from Job Matrix is made through our official email address, official contact number, or our verified WhatsApp Business account. If any person contacts you claiming to represent Job Matrix and requests payment through unofficial channels, it may be a fraudulent attempt.

Job Matrix will never ask candidates to share sensitive information such as OTPs, bank account passwords, card details, or confidential financial information. Users are strongly advised not to share such information with anyone claiming to represent Job Matrix. If you receive any suspicious requests of this nature, please avoid responding and report the incident immediately.

In case a candidate encounters any individual or website using the Job Matrix name fraudulently, we request users to immediately inform our team through our official contact channels. Candidates are advised to take screenshots of the suspicious message, save the website link or URL, and share this information with us so that we can investigate the matter. Users are also encouraged to report fraudulent websites or profiles that misuse the Job Matrix brand.

Candidates should also verify job-related communication carefully. Official job offer letters are always issued directly by the respective company through their official communication channels. Job Matrix may assist with interview coordination, but the final job offer will always come from the hiring organization itself.

The official contact number and email address of Job Matrix are always available on our website. Candidates should communicate only through these verified channels for any queries, support, or reporting suspicious activities.

Job Matrix does not permit any unauthorized person, agent, or third party to collect payments, represent the company, or communicate with candidates on our behalf. Any such activity should be treated as fraudulent and reported immediately.

For your safety, always verify information through our official website before making any payments or sharing personal details.
