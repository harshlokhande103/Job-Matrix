Fraud Alert - Important Notice

At Job Matrix, we never ask candidates to make any payment outside our official platform.

Any payment made through unofficial sources, third-party agents, or individuals claiming to represent Job Matrix will not be accepted or recognized by us.

If any third party uses our company name and collects money from you in any way, Job Matrix will not be responsible for such transactions.

All official communication will only be done through our verified email addresses and official contact details.

If you receive any suspicious call, email, or message asking for money in our name, please report it to us immediately.

Your safety and trust are very important to us. Stay alert and always verify before making any payment.
