Cancellation & Refund Policy

Job Matrix is committed to providing high-quality career services. This policy outlines the conditions under which cancellations and refunds are handled for our various service plans.

Cancellations must be requested through our official support channels at least 24 hours before any scheduled consultation or training session. Prepaid plans are eligible for a partial refund if requested within 48 hours of purchase, provided no services have been rendered.

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. For postpaid plans, services rendered are non-refundable as they represent time and expertise provided by our consultants.
