Cancellation and Refund Policy

Please read the following Cancellation and Refund Policy carefully. By purchasing any service plan on the Job Matrix website, the candidate agrees to the terms mentioned below.


Service Initiation & Eligibility
Once a candidate visits the Job Matrix website and purchases a service plan, the service process is considered officially initiated. From this point onward, Job Matrix begins delivering the selected services as per the plan. Candidates are advised to carefully review all plan details before purchase, as the policy, timelines, and refund eligibility will be applicable from the moment the payment is successfully completed.


Cancellation Within 24-48 Hours (Full Refund)
Candidates can request cancellation at any time; however, if the cancellation request is raised within 24 to 48 hours of plan purchase and no services or benefits have been availed, they will be eligible for a full refund. In such cases, only a 4% payment gateway convenience fee (based on the purchased plan) will be deducted, and the remaining amount will be refunded in 5-7 bank working days.


Mid-Process Cancellation & Partial Refund
Candidates are allowed to cancel their service plan at any stage of the process, except after job application. If a candidate is not satisfied with the service and chooses to cancel after availing any benefit, a fixed cancellation charge of Rs. 2000 per candidate will be applied. The remaining amount, if applicable, will be refunded after deducting applicable charges as per the policy.


Refund Eligibility
If Job Matrix fails to provide the promised services within a period of 3 months from the date of purchase, the candidate will be eligible for a full refund. In such cases, only a 4% payment gateway convenience fee will be deducted, and the remaining amount will be refunded. This ensures transparency and commitment towards service delivery timelines.


No Refund After Job Application
Once a candidate proceeds with the job application process after completing the services, the plan will be considered fully utilized. In such cases, the candidate will not be eligible for any cancellation or refund under any circumstances. Candidates are advised to make informed decisions before proceeding to the job application stage, as refund eligibility ends at this point.


Candidate Responsibility & No-Show Policy
Candidates must actively participate in the process by responding to communication, attending scheduled sessions, and providing accurate information. If a candidate fails to respond, provides misleading details, or misses scheduled meetings or seminars, they will be marked as a "No Show." In such cases, only partial refund may apply after deducting applicable charges, and full refund eligibility will not be considered.


Cancellation Request Process
To initiate cancellation, candidates must fill out the official cancellation form available on the Job Matrix website. The request will be reviewed within 24 to 48 hours. After approval, the refund will be processed within 5 to 7 bank working days to the selected payment mode. Candidates must ensure that all payment details provided in the form are accurate, as Job Matrix will not be responsible for failed transactions due to incorrect information.


Incorrect Information & Liability
Candidates are required to provide complete and accurate information while filling out the cancellation form and during the service process. If incorrect or invalid details are submitted, resulting in refund failure or service disruption, the responsibility will lie entirely with the candidate. Job Matrix will not be held liable for any loss caused due to incorrect information provided by the candidate.


Unethical Activity & Policy Violation
If a candidate is found involved in any unethical, illegal, or fraudulent activity, including misuse of services or leakage of confidential information, Job Matrix reserves the right to terminate the candidate's profile immediately. In such cases, the candidate will not be eligible for any refund under any circumstances, and access to all services may be permanently restricted.


Mandatory Website-Based Cancellation
All cancellation and refund requests will only be processed through the official website. Candidates must visit the "Cancellation & Refund" section and click on the Click Here option to access the cancellation form. Requests made through any other medium will not be considered valid. The process will only begin after successful submission of the cancellation form as per policy guidelines.


Job Guarantee Refund Clause
In the event that a candidate completes all required sessions but does not receive a job offer within 3 months, they will be eligible for a full refund. However, a 4% payment gateway convenience fee (based on the purchased plan) will be deducted and the remaining amount will be refunded to the candidate as per the standard refund process defined by Job Matrix.


Company-Initiated Cancellation
If Job Matrix cancels any service plan due to operational, business, or unforeseen reasons, the candidate will be eligible for full refund. In such cases, only a 4% payment gateway convenience fee will be deducted, and the remaining amount will be refunded. This ensures fairness and accountability from the company's side in service delivery commitments.


Plan Validity & Rescheduling Policy
Each service plan is valid for a maximum period of 3 months from the date of purchase. Candidates are expected to complete all sessions within this timeframe. One reschedule is allowed as a goodwill gesture; however, repeated rescheduling or delays beyond 30 days may result in plan invalidation. Expired plans will not be eligible for refund or further claims under any circumstances.


Non-Transferable Services
All service plans offered by Job Matrix are strictly non-transferable. Once a plan is purchased, it is exclusively assigned to the registered candidate and cannot be transferred to another individual. Any attempt to transfer or misuse the plan may result in cancellation without refund and restriction from accessing future services.
