Privacy Policy

Job Matrix Consultancy

Job Matrix Consultancy respects the privacy of every candidate and user who interacts with our website and services. We are committed to maintaining transparency and protecting the personal information shared with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you register on our website, communicate with us, or use our consultancy services. By accessing or using our website, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.


In order to provide effective interview training and career assistance services, Job Matrix may collect certain personal and professional information from candidates during registration or communication. This information may include details such as your name, contact number, email address, educational qualifications, work experience information, and residential location. The purpose of collecting this information is to understand your professional background and evaluate your profile so that we can assist you with suitable job opportunities and career guidance according to your qualifications, experience, and career interests and job assistance based on candidate eligibility and available opportunities.


The information provided by candidates is used strictly for professional and service-related purposes. This includes communicating with candidates regarding interview training, career guidance, and job-related opportunities that match their profile. The information may also be used to support candidate registration, maintain service records, and assist with any documentation process required during professional interactions. Our goal is to ensure that candidates receive relevant support and guidance that helps them prepare effectively for job interviews and career opportunities.


Job Matrix Consultancy follows strict confidentiality practices when handling candidate information. We do not sell, rent, or distribute personal data to any unknown or unauthorized third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Candidate information is not shared directly with employers without the candidate's awareness. In certain cases, a candidate's professional profile may be shared with verified and genuine companies for job-related opportunities, but only with the candidate's permission and solely for the purpose of facilitating employment opportunities.


We take appropriate technical and administrative measures to safeguard all personal information stored within our systems. Our team follows responsible data handling practices to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure of candidate information. While we strive to maintain a secure environment for all users, it is important to understand that no digital platform can guarantee absolute security. Nevertheless, we continuously implement reasonable safeguards to ensure that all personal data shared with us remains protected.


Users maintain full control over their personal information. If at any time a candidate wishes to update, correct, modify, or request deletion of their personal information, they may contact Job Matrix through our official communication channels. Upon receiving such requests, we will make reasonable efforts to process them and update or remove the requested information where appropriate, in accordance with our data management practices.

"Job Matrix Consultancy charges only for interview training and preparation services. We do not charge candidates for job placements."

Cookies Policy

Job Matrix Consultancy may use cookies and similar technologies on its website to improve overall functionality and provide a better browsing experience for users. The use of cookies helps us monitor website performance, improve navigation, and enhance the overall user experience. In some cases, trusted third-party services such as website analytics tools may also use cookies to help us understand traffic patterns and improve our website functionality. These tools assist us in identifying areas where we can enhance our services and optimize the website experience for visitors.


Users have full control over cookies and may choose to manage, disable, or delete cookies through their browser settings at any time. However, disabling cookies may affect certain features or functionality of the website, and some sections of the website may not operate as intended without them.
