Policy Overview

Shipping & Delivery Policy

Simple, transparent information about how Job Matrix services are delivered.

Digital Services Only

No physical shipping is applicable

Job Matrix is a service-based platform and does not offer any physical products for shipping. Therefore, no physical shipping or delivery is applicable.

01

How Services Are Delivered

All services provided by Job Matrix, including interview training, resume building, career guidance, and mock interview sessions, are delivered digitally through online communication channels such as phone calls, email, or WhatsApp.

02

Service Start Timeline

Once a candidate purchases a service plan, our team will contact the candidate within 24-48 hours to initiate the service process.

03

Scheduling & Delivery

All sessions and services are scheduled and delivered as per mutual availability and planned timelines.

04

Shipping Charges

There are no shipping charges applicable for any of our services.

Important Note:

Since all Job Matrix offerings are digitally delivered services, candidates can access support without any courier, parcel, or doorstep delivery process.
